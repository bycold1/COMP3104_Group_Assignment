This course is designed to better prepare professionals entering the IT field.
Specifically, a field where servers, web apps and infrastructure will be core to
business needs. 
COMP 3104 will provide a hands-on experience through the
implementation of tried, tested and true DevOps technologies.
DevOps is a combination of software developers (dev) and operations (ops). 
It is defined as a software engineering methodology which aims to integrate 
the work of software development and software operations teams by facilitating a culture of collaboration and shared responsibility
